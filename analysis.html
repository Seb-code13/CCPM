How can I analyze the effectiveness of my projects?

The programme

To know the efficiency of your projects I created an excel program to know if the differents projets are going well
In the programme you have to fill in 3 colomns (writed in red)

To make it easier to fill in the columns, please :

- Copy the tasks in GANTTPROJECT
- Paste into the first “Task name” column of the excel file
- Display the duration by right-clicking on the GANTTPROJECT headers
- Move the field to the left
- Copy the “Duration” column
- Paste into second “Time estimated” column
- Fill in the Buffer Time estimated in the top
- Finally, throughout your project, fill in the actual time.

You can see the progress of your projects on the graphs as you go along.
